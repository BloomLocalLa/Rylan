FAQ

Common Questions

What areas do you serve?

We're based in Georgia and serve the entire state, including Atlanta, Savannah, Augusta, and surrounding areas. We're also available for destination events.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 6-12 months in advance for weddings, especially for peak season dates. However, we can sometimes accommodate last-minute bookings.

Do you take song requests?

Absolutely! We work with you to create a custom playlist and also take requests from guests during the event to keep everyone dancing.

What equipment do you use?

We use professional-grade sound systems and lighting equipment. Our setup is tailored to your venue size and event needs.
